## Numerical Methods Context

5. Course materials for **MATH 5030 (Numerical Methods)**.

## How These References Connect to This Project

- The SABR model specification and smile-model motivation come from Hagan et al.
- The conditional pricing step is built on the Black-Scholes European call formula.
- The benchmarking and estimator-comparison framework follows standard Monte Carlo and variance-reduction ideas discussed in Glasserman.
- The project implementation choices, timestep experiments, and report framing are aligned with the numerical methods focus of the course.
